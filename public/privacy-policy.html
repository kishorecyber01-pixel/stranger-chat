Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2025 · Effective immediately
StrangerChat is built on anonymity. We collect as little data as possible and never sell your information to anyone.

1. Who We Are

StrangerChat ("we", "us", "our") operates the website strangerchat.com — a platform that connects strangers for anonymous text and video chat.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the service:

  • IP Address — used for abuse prevention, rate limiting, and IP banning repeat violators. Not stored permanently unless you are banned.
  • Session Data — age verification and captcha status stored in a temporary server session. Expires within 24 hours.
  • Nickname & Interests — entered voluntarily on the chat page. Never stored after your session ends.
  • Chat Messages — messages are relayed in real-time between users and are not stored on our servers.
  • Reports — when you report a user, we log the reported socket/IP to enforce bans. No message content is stored.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require account registration or email addresses
  • We do not store chat logs or conversation history
  • We do not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels
  • We do not collect payment information (no premium features)
  • We do not collect your real name, location, or device identifiers beyond IP

4. Cookies & Storage

We use a single session cookie to remember your age verification status within a browsing session. This cookie contains no personal information and is deleted when your session ends or within 24 hours.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking tools.

5. Cloudflare Turnstile

We use Cloudflare Turnstile for bot prevention on entry. Cloudflare may collect minimal technical data (IP, browser signals) as part of this verification. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

6. How We Use Your Information

  • To match you with a random stranger
  • To relay messages between matched users in real-time
  • To detect and block spam, abuse, and policy violations
  • To permanently ban IPs with repeated community violations

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We may disclose information only:

  • If required by law or a valid legal process
  • To protect the safety of users or the public
  • To our hosting provider (for infrastructure operation only)

8. Data Retention

  • Session data: deleted within 24 hours
  • Chat messages: never stored
  • Banned IPs: retained as long as necessary to enforce the ban
  • Report counts: reset periodically

9. Children's Privacy

StrangerChat is strictly for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect any information from minors. If you believe a minor has accessed our platform, please contact us immediately at privacy@strangerchat.com.

10. Your Rights

Since we do not store personal data beyond IP addresses (for banned users), there is typically nothing to delete or export. If you believe we hold data about you and wish to request deletion, contact us at privacy@strangerchat.com.

11. Security

We use HTTPS for all connections, server-side session management, and real-time abuse detection. However, no system is 100% secure — please do not share personal information with strangers on this platform.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact

For privacy-related questions: privacy@strangerchat.com
