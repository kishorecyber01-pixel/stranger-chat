Community Guidelines
Last updated: June 2026 · These rules apply to all users

StrangerChat exists to connect people from around the world in a fun, safe, and respectful environment. These guidelines exist to protect everyone. Violating them will result in warnings, disconnection, or a permanent IP ban.

✅ The Golden Rules

✅ Do This
😊
Be respectful
Treat every stranger the way you'd want to be treated. Kindness costs nothing.
🌍
Embrace diversity
You'll meet people from different countries, cultures, and backgrounds. Keep an open mind.
🚨
Report bad behaviour
Use the Report button if someone makes you uncomfortable. You're helping protect the whole community.
🔌
Disconnect freely
Not feeling the chat? Hit Next. You never have to stay in a conversation you don't want.

🚫 Strictly Prohibited

❌ Never Do This
🔞
No sexual content involving minors — ever
This is an absolute zero-tolerance rule. Any user sharing, soliciting, or referencing sexual content involving anyone under 18 will be permanently banned and reported to authorities.
💀
No threats or harassment
Threatening language, doxxing, stalking, or targeted harassment of any kind is forbidden. This includes "KYS", death threats, and similar language.
🤬
No hate speech
Slurs, racist language, homophobic or transphobic content, or attacks based on race, religion, gender, nationality, or sexuality are strictly banned.
📵
No spam or flooding
Sending the same message repeatedly, flooding the chat, or using bots/scripts to automate messages is not allowed.
🔗
No soliciting personal information
Do not pressure others for their phone number, social media, address, or any personal details. Do not share your own contact info to lure others off-platform.
💸
No scams or impersonation
Do not solicit money, promote pyramid schemes, phishing links, or impersonate other people or organisations.
🌐
No malicious links
Do not share links to malware, .onion sites, illegal content, or any URL designed to harm, track, or deceive the recipient.

⚠️ Use Good Judgement

⚠️ Grey Areas
💬
Adult conversation
While this is an 18+ platform and adult topics are permitted between consenting adults, unsolicited explicit content or sexual aggression is not. If your partner is uncomfortable, stop.
🗣️
Strong language
Casual swearing between consenting users is tolerated. Directed abuse, insults, or attempts to provoke are not.
🎭
Roleplay
Creative roleplay is fine between willing participants. Any roleplay involving minors — even fictional — is a permanent ban.

⚖️ How We Enforce These Rules

Violation First Offence Repeat / Severe
Spam / repeated messages Warning Disconnected
Mild policy violation Warning + blocked Disconnected
Hate speech / threats Instant disconnect IP Banned
3+ reports from users Disconnected IP Banned
CSAM / child safety Permanent IP Ban + Report to authorities —

Our automated moderation catches many violations in real-time. The community Report button is your most powerful tool — 3 reports from different users results in an immediate ban.

👶 Age Policy

StrangerChat is strictly for users 18 years and older. By entering the site you confirmed your age. If we have reason to believe a user is underage, they will be banned immediately. If you suspect someone is a minor, please report them immediately.

📩 Contact & Appeals

If you believe you were banned unfairly, contact us at support@strangerchat.com with your approximate location and the date/time of the ban. We review all appeals within 48 hours.

To report illegal content or child safety concerns: safety@strangerchat.com
